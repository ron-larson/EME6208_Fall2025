Ron Larson - EME6208 ePortfolio

A collection of design and development projects for Fall 2025 EME6208 Designing Integrated Media Environments 1.

Welcome to my Fall 2025 EME6208 ePortfolio. I am taking this course as a part of the Graduate Certificate in Instructional Design curriculum. This portfolio showcases the design and development skills I have learned throughout this course. The purpose of this site is to document my work on projects ranging from image editing and web design to video production and animation.

I am also an Assistant Professor in Computer Programming at Tallahassee State College, where I teach courses in Computer Literacy, Computer Programming (in languages such as C++ and Python), Artificial Intelligence (such as Cloud Essentials, Machine Learning Foundations), and Management Information Systems. I am also a Computer Science Education doctoral student. Thank you for stopping by and I hope the portfolio helps to inspire you to create something for the greater good. Be safe.
