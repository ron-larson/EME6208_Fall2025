About Me

I am a former IT Consultant with over 28 years of industry experience and seven years of K-12 teaching experience. I now teach Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, and Cybersecurity at the collegiate level to help foster the next generation of IT-aware professionals, while advancing curriculum and practice through educational leadership.

I utilize project-based learning, extensive industry experience, and culturally sensitive pedagogy to foster student success. This e-portfolio showcases the multimedia projects I have completed as a doctoral student in the Computer Science Education program at the University of Florida.
